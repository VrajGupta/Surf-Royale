## Question

Structure (not economics — pricing/currency are out of scope): cosmetic categories (gun skins, board designs, wetsuits — anything else?), how account levels earn unlocks, battle-pass tier count and XP sources, seasonal cadence, and the hard "no P2W / no gameplay effect" guarantees stated as testable rules (e.g. wetsuit color may not affect visibility in water).

## Decision (2026-07-17)

Launch cosmetics = base 3 only (gun skins, boards, wetsuits). Account levels: item every level, signature cosmetic at milestones 10/25/50/100; XP from match performance only. Battle pass: 50 tiers, XP from time + placement + kills + surf challenges, casual-completable (~4 matches/day). Seasons ~10 weeks, map/weather themed. No-P2W as testable triad: visibility tolerance band at 50m+, identical hitboxes across cosmetics, zero audio deltas — acceptance checks, not principles.
